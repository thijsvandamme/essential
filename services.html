What we do

Air Ambulance companies, medical assistance companies, travel insurances, hospital case managers, healthcare providers and many more turn to Gateway International EMS when they need to arrange ground transportation for their patients, medical teams, or accompanying family members.


“Worldwide EMS Solutions, Simplified!” ™



Clients rely on our unique services to assist them in transporting their patients and or medical crews by utilizing our global network of audited ground ambulances, wheelchair vans, town cars or limousines.

Whether it is medical or non-medical transportation clients put their trust in our company to ensure safe transfers to and from hospitals, airports and other locations. We relieve our clients of the hassle of organizing ground transportation, finding adequate providers and achieving negotiated rates.

Living by our slogan “Worldwide EMS Solutions, Simplified!” ™ our clients appreciate the diversity of our organization, offering individually customized solutions and our “hands-on & no bureaucracy” approach. We understand the need for quick, dependable and cost-efficient solutions. We engage our customers and provide interactive solutions and ensure transparency. It is our responsibility to offer the best possible solutions to our customers, both from a price and a quality perspective. Concentrating our efforts in this niche market segment allows us to provide our customers with only the best in service and quality.

Why us


Our round the clock service


Operating globally we ensure our stakeholders have access to our services 24/7/ 365 days a year. Our round the clock service has language capabilities of English, French, German and Italian. You will never hear automated responses or receive automated emails, we keep things personal and make sure each step of working with our company is interactive.

Global Network


Our audited network of providers ensure your patients, teams and family members will be transported safely anywhere in this world. Each provider is checked for good standing and required to complete a self-assessment and provide business licenses. Written contracts and agreements with our providers allow not only for smooth operations but also enable negotiated rates and hence empower us to offer you the best quality at best pricing!

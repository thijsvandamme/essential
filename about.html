About

Gateway International & Emergency Medical Services LLC provides a variety of specialized services in niche markets. Our organization provides comprehensive services to the air ambulance, medical and travel assistance industry as well as to corporations.

We have found that focusing on a few things but doing them all the more diligently and specializing in those fields allow us to provide our customers with only the best in service quality. Living by our slogan Worldwide EMS Solutions, Simplified!, our clients appreciate the diversity of our organization, offering individual and corporate solutions and our hands-on, no bureaucracy approach.

Our Vision

To surpass our limits and invest in global networks that bring together comprehensive resources in order to fulfill the requirements and exceed the expectations of our clients.

Our Mission

Gateway International & Emergency Medical Services LLC endeavors to be number one in bringing top notch customer service to its clients and their patients. Bringing quality care to quality customers.
